Volt is a distributed Tier-3 inference fabric across 25+ metro pods. Multi-vendor GPU, zero egress, Kubernetes-native. Three products under one stack:

- **⚡ Spark** — tokens-as-a-service. Llama 70B at **$0.95/M** standard, **$1.45/M** sovereign tier.
- **🔥 Forge** — GPU-as-a-service. NVIDIA B200 at **$2.36/GPU/hr** on 36-month reserved — 31% below CoreWeave list.
- **🔒 Vault** — dedicated bare-metal. 8-GPU B200 rack from **$85K/mo** on 36-month. Sovereign by default.

### Why Volt

- **Zero egress.** No ingress, no egress, no inter-pod transfer. Your data stays in the metro.
- **Cryptographic attestation.** Measured-boot proof of where your workload ran.
- **Multi-vendor.** NVIDIA today, AMD MI355X and Intel Gaudi 3 as the stack hardens.
- **CNCF-native.** Built on llm-d, KServe, Kueue, Cilium, and SPIRE — and we contribute back upstream.
